What is Deep Fake Detection?

Deep Fake Detection is a technology used to identify synthetically generated videos, images, or audio created using artificial intelligence. Deep fakes can manipulate reality by superimposing faces or altering voices, making it difficult to distinguish real from fake content.

Modern AI algorithms analyze features such as blinking patterns, inconsistencies in lighting, and unnatural facial movements to determine whether a media file has been altered.
